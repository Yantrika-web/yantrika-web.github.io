About Us

Welcome to YANTRIKA, the annual IT Fest organized by Naipunnya School of Management. Scheduled to take place on March 30, 2026 in our prestigious college auditorium, YANTRIKA is a celebration of technology, innovation, and creativity. YANTRIKA will feature thrilling competitions, insightful tech talks, and interactive workshops designed to inspire and ignite the passion for technology among participants. Let's make YANTRIKA 2K26 a memorable experience!
