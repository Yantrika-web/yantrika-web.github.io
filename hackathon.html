Hackathon rules

  • 1 Team size: 2–4 members. Solo not allowed.
  • 2 Theme will be announced at the start. You have 24 hours to build your solution.
  • 3 Tech stack: any language, framework, or tools. No restrictions.
  • 4 Code must be original. Use of open-source libraries allowed (credit where due).
  • 5 Submission via GitHub/zip with README, demo video (optional), and presentation.
  • 6 Bring your own laptops, chargers, and any hardware if needed. Internet provided.
  • 7 Judging criteria: innovation, technical complexity, impact, and presentation.
Prizes : 1st ₹40k · 2nd ₹25k · 3rd ₹15k + swag & incubation opportunities
