BGMI rules

  • 1 Team formats: Squad (4 players) or Duo (2 players). All members must be from same institution.
  • 2 Match format: TPP · Custom room. Map rotation announced before each round. no emulators
  • 3 Bring your own device (phone/tablet) and stable internet. Earphones allowed.
  • 4 Strictly no third-party tools, mods, or hacks. Fair play enforced – any violation leads to immediate disqualification.
  • 5 Match schedule: knockout rounds → semi‑finals → finals. Check your email for room ID and password.
  • 6 Check‑in mandatory 30 minutes before match start. Late entries will not be accommodated.
  • 7 Organisers' decision is final. Any form of cheating/toxicity results in ban from the tournament.
Prizes (per category): 1st ₹30k (Squad) / ₹15k (Duo) · 2nd ₹20k / ₹10k · 3rd ₹10k / ₹5k + in‑game rewards
